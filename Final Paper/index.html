A Societal Mission

Air University was founded almost 21 years ago on a bedrock of societal purpose. Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and contribution with integrity; advancing fundamental knowledge and cultivating creativity; leading in pioneering research for effective clinical therapies; and accelerating solutions and amplifying their impact.

Academic

Students' professional development and foundation building are both facilitated by their undergraduate education. Our diverse UG programmes turn students into successful people.

Graduate programmes encourage students to be inquisitive and enhance their knowledge and expertise in specialised sectors. As a result, AU has the most MS-level programmes available.

Thus, the goal of the PhD programmes at AU is to provide information that fuels economic progress. However, AU sought to produce knowledge for professional development.
